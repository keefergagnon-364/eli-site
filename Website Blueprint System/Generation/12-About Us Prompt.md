# 12 - About Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the About Section for the website.

The About Section should help visitors:

* Trust the business
* Understand the company's story
* Connect with the people behind the business
* Understand the company's experience and values

The About Section is not simply a biography.

Its primary purpose is to build trust and credibility.

---

# Required References

Review and follow:

* [[generalInstructions]]
* [[Ideal Website Standards]]
* [[03-Website Strategy]]
* [[04-Project Scope]]

Do not generate content until all referenced documents have been reviewed.

---

# About Section Priorities

Ranked in order of importance:

1. Build Trust
2. Tell Company Story
3. Humanize Business
4. Showcase Experience
5. Improve SEO
6. Differentiate From Competitors

---

# Required Elements

Whenever available include:

✓ Years Experience

✓ Mission Statement

✓ Company Values

---

# Optional Elements

Include when available:

✓ Owner Story

✓ Team Information

✓ Community Involvement

---

# About Section Objectives

The visitor should leave the section understanding:

* Who the company is
* Why the company exists
* Why they can be trusted
* What values guide the business

The visitor should feel more confident about contacting the business.

---

# Content Rules

Focus on:

* Trust
* Experience
* Reliability
* Professionalism
* Customer Commitment

Avoid:

* Excessive history lessons
* Long biographies
* Generic mission statements
* Large walls of text

Keep content concise and easy to scan.

---

# Storytelling Rules

When company history is available:

Explain:

* Why the business was started
* What motivates the team
* How the company serves customers

The story should reinforce trust.

The story should not become the primary focus.

---

# Trust Rules

Highlight trust-building information whenever available.

Examples:

* Years Experience
* Licensed & Insured
* Certifications
* Community Involvement
* Customer Satisfaction
* Team Expertise

Only use information supported by project documents.

Never invent credentials or experience.

---

# SEO Requirements

When possible:

* Include primary service keywords naturally
* Include service area references naturally
* Support local SEO objectives

Do not sacrifice readability for keywords.

---

# Image Recommendations

Prioritize:

1. Team Photos
2. Owner Photos
3. Project Photos
4. Community Photos
5. High Quality Stock Photos

Real photos should always be preferred.

Avoid generic corporate imagery.

Before finalizing:

☐ Trust is improved

☐ Company story is included appropriately

☐ Experience is highlighted

☐ Mission is clear

☐ Values are clear

☐ Content is concise

☐ SEO opportunities considered

☐ Real imagery recommended

☐ Supports Website Strategy

---

# Failure Conditions

The About Section fails review if:

* Trust is not improved
* Content focuses only on company history
* Generic mission statements are used
* Excessive text is present
* Experience is ignored
* Values are missing
* SEO opportunities are ignored

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
