# 10 - HERO Section Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the Hero Section for the website.

The Hero Section is the most important section of the website and must immediately communicate:

* Who the business is
* What the business does

Visitors should understand the business within 5 seconds.

The Hero Section should encourage visitors to continue scrolling and take action.

# Hero Requirements

Every Hero Section must include:

✓ Business Name

✓ Headline

✓ Subheadline

✓ Primary CTA

✓ Secondary CTA (Call Now)

✓ Background Image Recommendation

---

# Hero Priorities

Ranked in order of importance:

1. Encourage scrolling
2. Explain the business immediately
3. Look professional and visually impressive
4. Generate quote requests
5. Support SEO
6. Build trust

---

# Headline Rules

The headline should:

* Clearly explain what the business does
* Be easy to understand
* Be customer focused
* Avoid clever wordplay
* Avoid vague slogans

Whenever possible naturally include the primary keyword.

Bad Example:

"Bringing Your Vision To Life"

Good Example:

"Professional Tree Removal Services In Show Low"

---

# Subheadline Rules

The subheadline should:

* Support the headline
* Explain benefits
* Reinforce trust
* Remain concise

Avoid:

* Large paragraphs
* Generic marketing language
* Excessive buzzwords

---

# CTA Rules

Primary CTA:

Generate Quote Request focused options.

Examples:

* Get A Free Quote
* Request An Estimate
* Get Started Today

Secondary CTA:

Call Now

The CTA should be visible without scrolling.

---

# Trust Rules

If trust assets are available, recommend trust indicators.

Examples:

* Licensed & Insured
* 20+ Years Experience
* 500+ Projects Completed
* 4.9 Star Rating

Only use information supported by project documents.

Never invent credentials or statistics.

---

# Background Image Rules

Recommend imagery that:

* Represents the business
* Builds trust
* Supports the service being offered

Prioritize:

1. Real client photos
2. Real project photos
3. Team photos
4. High-quality stock photos

Avoid generic or unrelated imagery.

---

# SEO Requirements

When possible:

* Include primary keyword naturally
* Include service area naturally
* Support local SEO objectives

Do not sacrifice clarity for keywords.

---

# Failure Conditions

The Hero Section fails review if:

* The business is not immediately understood
* CTA is unclear
* Headline is vague
* Excessive text is present
* Generic marketing language is used
* Trust opportunities are ignored
* SEO opportunities are ignored

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
