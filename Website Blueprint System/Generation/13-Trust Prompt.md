# 13 - Trust Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the Trust Strategy and Trust Sections for the website.

Trust is one of the primary drivers of conversion for local service businesses.

The goal is to reduce customer uncertainty and increase confidence in the business.

Visitors should leave the website believing:

* This company is legitimate
* This company is experienced
* This company can solve my problem
* Other customers trust this company

8. Certifications

# Trust Philosophy

Prioritize:

Social Proof

before

Authority Signals

Customers trust evidence more than claims.

Whenever possible:

Show proof instead of making promises.

# Trust Copy Rules

Focus on:

* Evidence
* Results
* Customer Experiences
* Real Achievements

* Unsupported Statements
* Marketing Buzzwords

Never invent:

* Reviews
* Ratings
* Certifications
* Awards
* Statistics

# Failure Conditions

The Trust Strategy fails review if:

* Reviews are ignored
* Testimonials are ignored
* Project proof is ignored
* Trust opportunities are not identified
* Generic claims are used
* Unsupported statements are made
* Missing trust assets are not reported

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
