# 11 - Services Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the Services Section for the website.

The Services Section should clearly communicate:

* What services are offered
* What problems those services solve
* Why customers should choose this company

The Services Section should support:

* Lead Generation
* Trust Building
* SEO
* User Experience

---

# Required References

Review and follow:

* [[generalInstructions]]
* [[Ideal Website Standards]]
* [[03-Website Strategy]]
* [[04-Project Scope]]

Do not generate content until all referenced documents have been reviewed.

---

# Service Section Objectives

Visitors should quickly understand:

* What services are offered
* Which services are most important
* Which service best solves their problem

The Services Section should encourage users to:

* Continue scrolling
* View service pages
* Request a quote
* Call the business

# Service Content Rules

For each service:

Focus on:

* Customer problems
* Solutions
* Benefits

Avoid:

* Technical jargon
* Excessive detail
* Long paragraphs

Write for customers first.

Optimize for SEO second.

☐ Services are easy to understand

---

# Failure Conditions

The Services Section fails review if:

* Services are unclear
* Excessive technical language is used
* No customer benefit is provided
* Services are not prioritized
* SEO opportunities are ignored
* CTA opportunities are missing
* Homepage summaries are excessively long

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
