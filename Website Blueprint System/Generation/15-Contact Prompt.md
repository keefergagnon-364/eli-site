# 15 - Contact Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the Contact Section for the website.

The Contact Section is the final conversion opportunity and should make it as easy as possible for visitors to take action.

The primary goal is to encourage:

1. Quote Requests
2. Phone Calls
3. Contact Form Submissions
4. Email Contact
5. Booking Requests
6. Visit Location

The Contact Section should reduce friction and increase confidence.

# Contact Philosophy

The Contact Section should function as a final sales section.

Visitors should feel:

* Confident
* Comfortable
* Ready to take action

The section should reinforce trust and make contacting the business feel simple.

# Trust Reinforcement

Recommend trust elements near the contact form.

Examples:

* Licensed & Insured
* Years Experience
* Reviews
* Testimonials
* Response Time
* Satisfaction Guarantee

Only use verified information.

Never invent credentials or statistics.

---

# CTA Rules

Generate CTA options focused on action.

Examples:

* Request A Free Quote
* Get An Estimate
* Get Started Today
* Call Now

The CTA should feel easy and low-risk.

# Failure Conditions

The Contact Section fails review if:

* Contact information is difficult to find
* Quote request process is unclear
* CTA is weak
* Trust opportunities are ignored
* Form is unnecessarily complicated
* Mobile users face friction
* Contact process feels high-risk

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
