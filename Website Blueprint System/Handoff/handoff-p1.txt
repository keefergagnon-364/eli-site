Document Purpose

This document is the authoritative source for the implementation of the IVY Remodeling website.

The planning phase has been completed. All strategic decisions have been reviewed, approved, and frozen.

The purpose of this handbook is to provide the production team with every decision necessary to build the website without consulting the planning documents.

This document replaces all previous planning documents during development.

If a conflict exists between previous planning documents and this handbook, this handbook takes precedence.

The development team is responsible for implementing the approved design—not redesigning, re-planning, or changing business strategy.

Primary Business Objective

Generate qualified remodeling leads through an attractive, professional, high-performing website that communicates trust, craftsmanship, and professionalism.

The website exists primarily to convert visitors into estimate requests.

Everything else is secondary.

Customer Psychology

Visitors typically arrive with one or more concerns:

Can I trust this contractor?
Will they communicate well?
Will they show up?
Will the work last?
Are they licensed and insured?
Can I see examples of their work?
Is this worth the investment?

The website should answer these questions progressively through content, imagery, trust signals, and project examples rather than relying on claims alone.
