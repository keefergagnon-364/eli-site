# 21 - SEO & Local Visibility Prompt

## Purpose

Generate the SEO and Local Visibility Strategy for the website.

The goal is to improve:

* Search Visibility
* Local Visibility
* Service Discovery
* Lead Generation

The objective is not simply ranking for keywords.

The objective is helping local customers find the business when searching for relevant services.

# SEO & Local Visibility Priorities

Ranked in order of importance:

1. Google Business Profile
2. Local Keywords
3. Reviews
4. Service Pages
5. Technical SEO
6. Schema Markup
7. Location Pages
8. Blog Content
9. Citations (Directory Listings)

Backlinks should be considered when valuable but are not a primary focus for most local service businesses.

---

# SEO Philosophy

Prioritize:

Local Visibility

before

Traditional SEO

The goal is:

* More Calls
* More Quote Requests
* More Local Customers

Not vanity rankings.

# Failure Conditions

The strategy fails review if:

* Local visibility is ignored
* Google Business Profile is ignored
* Reviews are ignored
* Service page opportunities are ignored
* Technical SEO is ignored
* Location pages are recommended without justification
* Blog content is recommended without a business case
* Strategy does not support lead generation

Revise until all failure conditions are resolved.
